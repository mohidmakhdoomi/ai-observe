This is a **meaningful improvement** over the plan's original `except ImportError` suggestion. Catching `ModuleNotFoundError` and checking `exc.name` ensures:

- Import-time errors in installed code (e.g., `SyntaxError`) → not caught at all ✓

Lazy imports (`sys`, `Path` inside the `except` block) keep the installed path zero-overhead. Docstrings explain the two-path behavior clearly.
